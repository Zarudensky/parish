Volunteer Sign Up

Make it easy for parishioners to sign up for activities at your church
with Sign Up. No matter how complex your needs our, Sign Up will
make it easy for you to setup and your parishioners to get signed
up. From ministries to bake sales to community socials, Sign Up is
your go to tool to line up the help you need.

Membership Made Easy

Parish Home brings modern ease of use, speed, and flexibility to
member management. Our powerful search helps you locate family
records in seconds and easily maintain demographics, life events,
and Sacraments. More than just a maintenance tool, Parish Home
helps you understand how members are engaged
with your community through time, talent, and treasure.

Powerful Donation Management

Donations are critical to your mission and Parish Home gives you
the tools you need to manage, track and report member contributions
with efficiency and accuracy. Entry of weekly checks is snap with our
bulk entry tool, plus you can accept online donations or easily import
donations from third-party systems. Customizable statements can
be generated at any time or you can allow members to access
their own statements online.

Ministries

Our ministry communications feature makes it easy for members to engage
with your ministries and keep on top of everything that’s happening.
Instantly form ministry groups that include messaging, a dedicated calendar,
and file sharing. Because it is fully integrated with our membership
database, there is no need to sync accounts between your church
management system and ministry communications.

Automated Message Translation

It can be very difficult for members to engage with your community when
language is a barrier. That’s why Parish Home includes automated
bi-directional translation for all group communications. Members can
select from over 100 languages and messages sent to them will be
translated to their desired language. Conversely, messages they send will
be translated from their language
to English – or the selected language of the recipients.

Faith Formation

Organize your faith formation classes and manage everything from
registration, with optional payment, through class sessions. Each
class includes communications, a class calendar and file sharing.
Features such as attendance, reminders, and message translation
help keep everyone in the loop and reduces missed classes.
Classes are available through our mobile apps and website.

Events and Event Registration

Parish Home Events bring together our event calendar, facilities
management, and custom registration forms to give you a single, simple
way to setup and manage events. Create events on your facilities calendar
to reserve the facility. Add a custom registration page, with optional
payment, to allow parishioners to signup.

Facilities Scheduler

Streamline your facilities event calendar management and open new
possibilities with our powerful facilities management system. You can allow
members to make reservation requests online with automated approval
workflows. Have underutilized resources? Rentals can fill the gaps and provide a
welcome revenue stream. We support iCal, widgets, or custom data feeds so
you can display a professional looking calendar on your own website.
