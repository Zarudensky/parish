Helping you get the most out of Parish Home

We want you to love your experience with Parish Home and we’ll be with you every step of the way to
make sure you get off on the right foot.

Data conversion and implementation

Our conversion specialists will help you get your data from your
existing system and into Parish Home. Move a little or move it all,
it’s up to you. We’ll work with you to define the settings that work
best for your community and will help you get all your
administrative staff up and running quickly.

Training

We love helping people learn to use Parish Home and offer on-site
or web-based training for your staff and ministries. Parish Home is
very easy to use, so most training is quick, but we’re always
available to follow up or answer any questions that come up.

Consulting

We’ve been fortunate to work with some terrific parishes and
have learned a great deal along the way. Our consulting will
help you maximize the benefits of Parish Home and best
practices for the application of technology
throughout your ministry.

Support

We make it easy to get help whenever you need it.
Reach out by phone or email and get rapid
answers to your questions or problems.
